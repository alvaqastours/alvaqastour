AL VAQAS TOURS & TRAVELS PRIVATE LIMITED

AL VAQAS TOURS & TRAVELS PRIVATE LIMITED is a trusted name for Umrah and Hajj tours. We guide pilgrims with full care and respect, knowing how important this journey is for every believer. From the moment you book with us, we make sure all arrangements are smooth and easy. Our goal is to give you a safe, comfortable, and peaceful experience so you can focus on your prayers.

To make your travel worry-free, we also provide fresh and hygienic food at the airport before departure. We arrange Ziyarat trips to all important holy sites, allowing you to visit and pray with comfort. Reliable transport is always ready to take you wherever needed, ensuring your journey is stress-free. Every step is planned in advance so you can travel with peace of mind.

We believe that everyone should be able to perform their pilgrimage at a fair price. That is why we offer different packages, such as economy and silver, to fit your needs and budget. With our best pricing and quality service, you will always get full value for your money. Traveling with us means comfort, honesty, and care throughout your sacred journey.
