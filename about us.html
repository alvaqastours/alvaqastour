Journey Of Heart

🕌Our Mission

At AL VAQAS TOURS & TRAVELS PRIVATE LIMITED, we understand that performing Umrah and Hajj is one of the most sacred and important journeys in a Muslim's life. Our mission is to make this spiritual journey as smooth, comfortable, and meaningful as possible for every pilgrim who trusts us with their sacred pilgrimage.

We are dedicated to providing exceptional service with the utmost care and respect, ensuring that your focus remains on your spiritual devotion while we handle all the logistics and arrangements.

⭐Why Choose Us

With years of experience in organizing religious tours, we have built a reputation as a trusted name in the industry. Our team understands the significance of your pilgrimage and works tirelessly to ensure every detail is perfectly arranged.

From the moment you book with us until you return home, we provide comprehensive support, reliable transportation, comfortable accommodations, and complete peace of mind throughout your sacred journey.

🍽️Complete Care

We believe in providing holistic care to our pilgrims. This includes arranging fresh and hygienic meals at the airport before departure, ensuring you start your journey well-nourished and comfortable.

Our comprehensive Ziyarat trips cover all important holy sites, allowing you to visit and pray at these sacred locations with complete comfort and proper guidance from our experienced team.

🚐Reliable Transportation

We understand that reliable transportation is crucial for a stress-free pilgrimage. Our fleet of well-maintained vehicles and experienced drivers are always ready to take you wherever you need to go, ensuring your journey is safe and comfortable.

Every aspect of transportation is planned in advance, so you can travel with complete peace of mind, knowing that all arrangements are in place.

💰Fair Pricing

We firmly believe that everyone should have the opportunity to perform their pilgrimage at a fair and reasonable price. That's why we offer various packages, including economy and silver options, designed to fit different needs and budgets.

With our competitive pricing and quality service, you will always receive excellent value for your investment, ensuring your sacred journey is both affordable and memorable.

🤝Our Commitment

Our commitment extends beyond just booking your trip. We provide end-to-end support with honesty, transparency, and genuine care. Our team is available to assist you at every step, ensuring that your pilgrimage experience exceeds your expectations.

We take pride in the trust our clients place in us and work continuously to maintain our reputation as a reliable and caring travel partner for your most important spiritual journey.
